Touriga Nacional

Touriga Nacional is of low production: it has many, but small bunches. The berries have a high concentration of sugar, colour and aromas. Wines produced or blended with this grape variety are very balanced, alcoholic and have a good ageing potential.

Touriga Franca

Touriga Franca has regular productions throughout the year and is very resistant to diseases. Its bunches are medium-sized or big with round, medium-sized berries. Wines produced by this grape variety have a deep colour and are very fruity.

Tinta Roriz (Tempranillo)

In order for the Aragonez qualities to be excellent, its production has to be controlled. Ideal are sandy and carbonate-clayey soils in hot and dry climates, to make the production lower and the berries more concentrated. This grape variety produces wines with high alcohol content and low acidity, ideal for ageing, as they are very resistant to rust.

Tinta Barroca

You will rarely meet it as a single variety, but it forms part of most red Douro blends, contributing dark colour without too much tannin, thanks to its dark but thin skins, along with plummy, cherry fruit.
