Salvador Vineyard

Our Salvador vineyards account for 20ha and produce Alvarinho, Loureiro, Avesso, Vinhão and Arinto with soils of crushed granite. This vineyards benefits from a altitude of 350m and a Northern exposure which captures the rising and setting sun.

Vale e Pena Vineyard

A 14 hectares vineyard consisting of Vinhão, loureiro, azal, arinto and alvarinho. Hillside vineyards with granite soils and ocean breezes with western sun exposure helping to make up the terroir. This vineyard contributes to the production of each level of our portfolio. Terraced vineyards

Locaia Vineyard

A small and precious vineyard planted alongside the river with 2,5 ha in size andproducing Trajadura and Azal.

Calçada Vineyard

The main Estate vineyard with nearly 4ha surrounds the hilltop of Quinta da Calçada. Terraced vineyards built originally in the 1910’s in granitic soils. A small portion of the original vineyards are certified as the oldest in the region of vinhos verdes.

Varzea Vineyard

The last vineyard frontier of Calçada’s Estate with 14ha rests in a river valley at the base of the Marao mountain range. The defining characteristic of this vineyard is the winds originating from the ocean, creating an updraft across the lower plateau of this river valley vineyard. The varietals planted here are Loureiro and arinto usually originating most of our sparkling wines.
